Installation & Commissioning Services

Surya Tech Solutions specializes in the professional installation and commissioning of telecom infrastructure, including BTS, MSC/BSC multiplexers, and optical fiber networks. Our engineers ensure precise deployment, proper configuration, and seamless integration with existing networks.

Each project is executed following standardized telecom procedures, guaranteeing uninterrupted connectivity, optimized network performance, and full compliance with industry standards.

Benefits of This Service

  • Accurate BTS and multiplexer setup
  • Optimized telecom network performance
  • Reliable fiber splicing and testing
  • Minimized network downtime
  • Compliance with telecom standards
  • Scalable for future network expansions
  • Experienced field engineering support

Our team provides end-to-end support, from planning and installation to post-commissioning testing and optimization, ensuring a reliable and future-ready telecom network.
